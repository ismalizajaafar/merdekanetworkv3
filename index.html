Welcome to Merdeka Network

Merdeka Network Sdn Bhd, established in 2022, is a forward-thinking and rapidly growing company with a dual focus on two major sectors: Engineering and Medical. With an initial paid-up capital of RM 10 million, we have positioned ourselves for rapid expansion, aiming to provide world-class solutions that cater to the evolving needs of our clients.

Merdeka Network strives to provide exceptional engineering services and healthcare solutions that stand at the forefront of technological and medical advancements.
